How We Can Help You

Services

We are a small but growing company serving small firms who require affordable but effective IT solutions.

Oracle Database Management

If you run or plan to implement Oracle products and you require honest and efficient support, please contact us for a detailed discussion of your requirements. We could even help you formulate your requirements if you find the Oracle technology jungle a little overwhelming.

Oracle Application Server Deployment

We can implement Oracle options such as Data Guard, RAC, or RMAN. Not only would you have peace of mind knowing these strategies are in place, we can offer full operational support either on-site or remotely.

Remote DBA services

If you are looking to supplement your current system with a remote DBA service, we can provide comprehensive coverage in areas of 24X7 monitoring, issue resolution and proactive performance management.
